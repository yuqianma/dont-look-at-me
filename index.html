/* Don't Look At Me When I'm Coding */
Video by Renton

Know More: Slides | PDF

On the black screen, you'll see a live depiction of the creator coding. However, it stops when observed — expressing the creator's discomfort when someone is watching him code.

On the white screen, the code is output by AI, but only outputs when you are watching it.

This installation seeks to discuss the place and emotions of individuals in their work, regardless of the nature of the job or the era.

在黑色屏幕上，你会看到创作者实时编程的呈现。然而，当被观察时，它会停止 — 表达出创作者不愿被人看着编程。

在白色屏幕上，代码由 AI 输出，但只有被你看着的时候才输出。

本装置想讨论工作中人的地位和情绪，无论什么工作，什么时代。
